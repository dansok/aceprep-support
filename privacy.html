AcePrep Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

Overview

AcePrep ("the App") is developed by Dan Sokolsky. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

Data Collection

AcePrep does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All data — including your progress, streaks, bookmarks, and preferences — is stored locally on your device.

Analytics

The App does not use third-party analytics services. No usage data is shared with third parties.

In-App Purchases

Subscription purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. AcePrep does not have access to your payment information.

Children's Privacy

AcePrep does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App is suitable for all ages and does not require account creation or personal data entry.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at locus.games13@gmail.com.
