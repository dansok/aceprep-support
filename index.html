FAQ

How does the adaptive learning work?

AcePrep tracks your performance across all 21 SAT categories and automatically focuses your practice on the areas where you need the most improvement.

How many free questions do I get?

Free users get 10 questions per day. AcePrep Premium unlocks unlimited questions and practice tests.

How do I restore my subscription?

Open the app, tap the upgrade button, then tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom of the screen.

Is my data stored online?

No. All your progress, streaks, and bookmarks are stored locally on your device.
